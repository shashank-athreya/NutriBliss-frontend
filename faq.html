Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NutriBliss orders.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for customers who prefer to pay after receiving the order.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using your phone number on the Track Order page.

Are online payments safe?

Yes, online payments are handled securely through Razorpay.

How fresh are the products?

NutriBliss focuses on fresh, quality-packed dry fruits for daily healthy snacking.

What if my payment succeeds but order is not visible?

Please contact us immediately through the Contact page with your phone number and payment reference.

Can I cancel my order?

You can contact us as soon as possible. If the order has not been shipped, we can help with cancellation.

Do you provide bulk orders?

Yes, for bulk or gifting orders, contact us through WhatsApp or phone.
