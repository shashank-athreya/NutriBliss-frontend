Shipping Policy

NutriBliss aims to deliver fresh and high-quality dry fruits to your doorstep in a timely manner.

Delivery Locations

We currently deliver across major locations in India. Availability may vary depending on your area.

Processing Time

Orders are usually processed within 24–48 hours after confirmation.

Delivery Time

Delivery typically takes 2–7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges may vary based on order value and delivery location. Any applicable charges will be shown during checkout.

Cash on Delivery

COD is available in selected areas. Additional charges may apply for COD orders.

Order Tracking

You can track your order anytime using your phone number on the Track Order page.

Delays

Delivery delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather, logistics issues, or high demand.

Contact

For any shipping-related queries, please contact us through the Contact page.
